Words-Level 2 Lesson

Words in English

For you to speak English,words are extremely important.The more words you know, the easier it will be for you to speak English

The question is-How many words do you need to know in English? This is a very common question and it varies depending on your goal.

If your goal is to speak English fluently,you are not required to study 2000 words

What is a Word?

A word converts the group of letters into a meaningful one. A word plays a key role in the Units of English.

A word is a group of letters with a meaning.

Examples- Ball,nation,ocean,boy,courage,install,save and organization are words.

The word ball is having four letters b..a..l..l.

The word organization is having four letters o..r..g..a..n..i..z..a..t..i..o..n.

In this way all the words are collective forms of few letters.

Every word carries a meaning.
